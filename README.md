# FUTURE_DS_01 – Business Sales Performance Analytics

## Internship
Completed as part of the Data Science & Analytics Internship under the Fellowship Program at Future Interns.

## Project Objective
To analyze business sales data and identify revenue trends, top-selling products, profitable categories, regional performance, and customer segment contribution.

## Tools Used
- Microsoft Power BI
- Microsoft Excel
- DAX
- Data Cleaning and Data Visualization

## Key KPIs
- Total Sales: $2.3M
- Total Profit: $286.4K
- Total Orders: 5K
- Total Quantity: 38K
- Average Order Value: $458.6
- Profit Margin: 12.47%

## Key Insights
- West region generated the highest profit.
- Technology category delivered the highest sales.
- Consumer segment contributed the largest share of total sales.
- A small number of products contributed a significant share of revenue.
- Profitability should be monitored for discount-heavy products.

## Dashboard Features
- Monthly sales trend analysis
- Top-selling product analysis
- Sales and profit comparison by category
- Regional profit analysis
- Customer segment contribution analysis
- Interactive filters for Year, Region, Category, and Segment
